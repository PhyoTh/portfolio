Where E is energy, m is mass, and c is the speed of light.

Why MathML took so long to be usable:

Even though MathML has been included in the HTML5 specification for quite some time, it hasn't really been practical to use until recently because many browsers didn't support it, especially the Chromium-based ones like Chrome, Edge, and Opera. While Firefox and Safari had offered support for MathML for years, it wasn't until January 2023, with the release of version 109, that Chrome finally added native support for it.
